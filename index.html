Why New York City Rocks

A small dedication to my favorite city.


The Big Apple gets a bad rap sometimes but it has more going for it than against. Let's explore why.
(Hint: Some of that is pizza.)

Whether it's the smell or the traffic, people find reasons to complain why New York City is a horrible place to live.
Well, they're wrong. They should start looking at all of the positives that drive people to somehow pay the crazy rent.
For example:

  • No car needed
  • Most things are within walking distance
  • Any type of food you can imagine

The city is made up of 5 boroughs and each has its own attractions. Here are just a few of my favorite:

Borough Attraction
Manhattan Grand Central Terminal, The Sheep's Meadow at Central Park, Doyer's Street
Brooklyn Williamsburg, McGlorick Park, DUMBO, Brooklyn Bridge Park
Queens MoMa PS1, Astoria, Chinatown, Ridgewood
The Bronx Arthur Avenue, Yankee Stadium, Woodlawn Cemetary
Staten Island Staten Island Ferry...that's all I can think of
