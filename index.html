Why New York City Rocks

A small dedication to my favorite city.


The Big Apple gets a bad rap sometimes but it has more going for it than against. Let's explore why. (Hint: Some of that is pizza.)

Whether it's the smell or the traffic, people find reasons to complain why New York City is a horrible place to live. Well, they're wrong. They should start looking at all of the positives that drive people to somehow pay the crazy rent. For example:

  • No car needed.
  • Most things are within walking distance
  • Any type of food you can imagine
